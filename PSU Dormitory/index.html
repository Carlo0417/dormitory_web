About the Dormitories

The Palawan State University Dormitory is composed of three buildings; for male, female, and foreign bonafide students studying in the Main Campus. The dorms has 61 rooms when combined and the dormitory offers facilities and services for the occupants that will determine their dormitory fees. The dormitory serve as a home for students living inside and outside Palawan and still feel home during their stay.

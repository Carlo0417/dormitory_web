Dormitory Goals

"To provide a place, where one can feel as if he/she is at home in an atmosphere of love, care, support and safety."



Dormitory Objectives

We, the residents, the dorm manager, the dorm parent and the staffs commit ourselves:

  • To build "home" away from home where every resident feels the essence of being part of the family.
  • To provide conducive environment for everybody to feel the essence of real home.
  • To foster camaraderie in a friendly atmosphere of acceptance and concern especially to the newcomers.
  • To provide approachable dormitory manager and parent for the student's queries, suggestions and needs.
  • To ensure safety and security within the dormitory.


Palawan State University Mission

Palawan State University is committed to upgrade people's quality of life by providing education opportunities through excellent instruction, research and innovation, extension, production services, transnational collaborations.



Palawan State University Vision

An internationally recognized university that provides relevant and innovative education and research for lifelong learning and sustainable development.




Where is it located?

The Palawan State University Dormitory is located inside the Palawan State University-Main Campus at Tiniguiban Heights, Barangay Tiniguiban, Puerto Princesa City. It is intended to provide bed space and a place to stay for the enrolled student of Palawan State University who come from municipalities, other provinces, and other countries.

Male Dormitory

The male dormitory consists of 21 rooms with 5 with 3 double-deck bed that can accommodate 6 occupants per room. Each room consists of ceiling fans, closet, table chairs for each of the occupant. There will be one room for LGBTQIA+ room in every floor. The dormitory for male has a study lounge, electric room, 2 common toilet and baths both on ground and second floor, utility kitchen, laundry area, dining area, sleeping area, storage room, and more.

Female Dormitory

The dormitory was designated for female is a 3-story building composed of 27 rooms with 3 double-beds inside good for 5-6 occupants. Each room has ceiling fans, closet, table, chair for each occupant. There is a designated room for LGBTQIA+. There will be a mess hall, kitchen, laundry area, comfort rooms in the ground floor. There is also a basement where there is a function and recreational hall.

Foreign Dormitory

Foreign dormitory is a 2-story which is composed of 3 rooms with 3 beds, own closet and a private toilet and bath. Also, an internet cafe, laundry area, comfort room, lobby, dining area, manager’s room, etc. The 2nd floor comprises 10 rooms with 3 beds, a closet, and a private toilet and bath. and waiting area, comprising 13 rooms in total. There will also be a designated room for LGBTQ+ occupants.
